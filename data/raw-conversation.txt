BÀI 1.
1. Peter said: "You've made a terrible mistake".
=> Peter told me (that) I had made a terrible mistake.
*Note: "that" có hay không có cũng được em nhé.
2. "I am leaving tomorrow", she said.
=> She said she was leaving the following day.
3. "Who did you give the money to?", my mother said.
=> My mother asked me who/whom I had given the money to.
=> My mother asked me to whom I had given the money.
4. The guide said: "Do you want to see the cathedral?".
=> The guide asked me if/whether I wanted to see the cathedral.
=>  The guide asked us if/whether we wanted to see the cathedral.
5. "We were waiting for the school bus at 8 o'clock yesterday", they said.
=> They said they had been waiting for the school bus at 8 o'clock the day before.
=> They said they had been waiting for the school bus at 8 o'clock the previous day.
6. The teacher said to me: "Why don't you answer my question?".
=> The teacher asked me why I didn't answer his/her question.
7. "I`ll come and see you off at the station", said Miss Mary.
=> Miss Mary said she would come and see me off at the station.
8. "How long have you been learning English?".
*Note: Xem lại câu này nhé, em viết thiếu rồi. Tôi thêm "he asked me" nhé.
=> He asked me how long I had been learning English.
9. My parents said: "You have to come home before 10 o`clock".
=> My parents said I had to come home before 10 o`clock".
10. "I can't go to the cinema with you, John", she said.
=> She told John she couldn't go to the cinema with him.
11. "I have studied French for several years", he said.
=> He said he had studied French for several years.
12. "Why are you late for class, Tom?", she said.
=> She asked Tom why he was late for class.
13. "Do you like learning English?", she said.
=> She asked me if/whether I liked learning English.
=> She asked us if/whether we liked learning English.
14. "Have you finished your exam?", she asked her students.
=> She asked her students if/whether they had finished their exam.
15. "I find it difficult to learn English well", she said.
=> She said she found it difficult to learn English well.
16. He said to me: "Do this exercise again, please".
=> He requested me to do that exercise again.
17. She asked him: "How old are you?".
=> She asked him how old he was.
18. My father said to us: "Don't forget to do your homework!".
=> My father asked us not to forget to do our homework.
19. John said: "Do you have any shampoo, Alice? I must wash my hair".
=> John asked Alice if/whether she had any shampoo because he had to wash his hair.

BÀI 2.
1. Minh said that his sister worked for a company in the city center.
=> "My sister works for a company in the city center", Minh said.
2. Mr.Phong said that he was learning English in an evening class.
=> "I am learning English in an evening class", Mr.Phong said.
3. The examiner asked Lan how she learned English in her country.
=> "How do you learn English in your country?", the examiner asked Lan.
4. Paula told Lan not to be late for the exam the following day.
=> "Don't be late for the exam tomorrow, Lan", Paula said.
=> Paula said to Lan: "Don't be late for the exam tomorrow!".
5. Nga asked Jane if she could speak Vietnamese.
=> "Can you speak Vietnamese?", Nga asked Jane.
6. Mrs.Hue asked me how far it was from my house to the school.
=> "How far is it from your house to the school?", Mrs.Hue asked me.
7. The students said that they were having a test the next Friday.
=> The students said: "We are having a test next Friday".
8. Trung asked the landlord how much the test was.
*Note: Sao lại là "test"? Có phải là "tax" hay là cái gì đó khác không? Xem lại nhé.
=> Trung asked the landlord: "How much is the test?".
